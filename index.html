Map of Armed and Unarmed Police Shootings

What this Map Shows?

This map shows the crowdsourced police shootings data retrieved from Deadspin Police Shooting Database last fall. It shows the locations of police shootings for armed and unarmed people. The information also consists of the date, race, whether the individual survived, and a summary of the incident.

Why it is Important?

This is some very sensitive information and it can be difficult to imagine where various police shootings can occur, so an interactive map can showcase a record of the shootings from a visual perspective. In this zoomed up view of my map, the blue marker indicates my city, and the blue polygon indicates the cities closest to where I live, where police shootings have occurred. This can help me compare the amount of police-shootings in other cities within the vicinity compared to mine. These cities include Woodinville, Bellevue, Seattle, and one is very close to where I live in Shoreline.
