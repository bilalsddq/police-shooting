Map of Armed and Unarmed Police Shootings

What this Map Shows?

This map shows the crowdsourced police shootings data retrieved from Deadspin Police Shooting Database last fall. It shows the locations of police shootings for armed and unarmed people. Armed shootings are indicated by a red handgun icon, and unarmed shootings are indicated by a blue palm icon. The information also consists of the date, race, whether the person survived, and a summary of the incident.

Why it is Important?

This is some very sensitive information and it can be difficult to imagine where various police shootings can occur, so an interactive map can showcase a record of the shootings from a visual perspective.
